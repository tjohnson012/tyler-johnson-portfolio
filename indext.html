Biography

I'm Tyler Jalon Johnson, a Computer Science student at the University of Cincinnati with a minor in Math. I am passionate about software engineering and planning for a career in fintech and enterprise-scale applications.

During my time in college, I have had the opportunity to work at companies like Synchrony, BetterLLC, and General Electric, gaining experience in everything from startup environments to enterprise-scale systems. I'm proud to be a Bearcat and embody the spirit of determination and excellence that our university represents.

As a Cincinnati Pride Scholar, Backrs Scholar, and University Honors Scholar, I'm committed to academic excellence while actively contributing to campus life as Chair of Campus Life in Student Body Government and Ambassador for the Warren Bennis Leadership Institute.

My interests include archival fashion, marathons, capoeira, language exchange, anime, gaming, and volunteering - always staying active in the UC community!
